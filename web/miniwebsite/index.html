welcome to vira college

The College was established in the year 1999 and is duly approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE),
Delhi and affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (formerly known as Uttar Pradesh Technical University), Lucknow.
The first batch of the college was passed out in the year 2003 and till date 19 batches have been pass out.
